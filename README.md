# 📊 Digital-Marketing-Performance-Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a **professional and interactive Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze digital marketing performance across campaigns, channels, and regions.

It enables data-driven decision-making by tracking key marketing KPIs, visualizing customer funnel behavior, and identifying performance gaps in engagement and conversion.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Track core marketing KPIs such as Impressions, Clicks, and Conversions
* Measure performance using CTR (Click-Through Rate) and CVR (Conversion Rate)
* Analyze campaign effectiveness across channels and regions
* Identify drop-offs in the marketing funnel (Impression → Click → Conversion)
* Evaluate ROI and Cost per Acquisition (CPA)

## 📊 Key Features

* **KPI Cards**: Total Impressions, Total Clicks, CPA, Weighted ROI, Avg Engagement
* **Funnel Analysis**: Visual breakdown of Impression → Click → Conversion performance
* **Channel Performance**: Distribution of impressions across marketing platforms
* **Geo Analysis**: Location-based performance using map visualization
* **Dynamic Filters**: Time period, marketing channel, region, and campaign type
* **Engagement Summary**: Quick insights on CTR, CVR, and audience retention

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Strong CTR indicates effective ad creatives and targeting
* Conversion rate highlights how well traffic turns into customers
* Significant drop-off between clicks and conversions suggests optimization opportunities
* Certain channels contribute more impressions but not necessarily conversions
* Geographic analysis reveals high-performing regions for targeted campaigns
